Getting Started

How to Play

Use the paddle to bounce the ball and destroy all the bricks. Don't let the ball fall below the screen!

🏓

Move the Paddle

Use your mouse, arrow keys, or finger (on mobile) to slide the paddle left and right along the bottom of the screen.

🔴

Break Bricks

The ball bounces off the paddle, walls, and bricks. Each brick destroyed earns points. Clear all bricks to advance.

❤️

Don't Miss!

You start with 3 lives. If the ball falls past your paddle, you lose a life. Lose all lives and it's game over.

⭐

Collect Power-Ups

Destroyed bricks may drop power-ups. Catch them with your paddle to activate special abilities.

🏆

Beat All 5 Levels

Each level has a unique brick layout and faster ball speed. Beat all five levels to win the game!

🎯

Aim Your Bounces

Where the ball hits the paddle affects the bounce angle. Hit the edges for sharp angles, center for straight bounces.

Abilities

Power-Ups

Destroyed bricks have a 25% chance to drop a power-up. Catch it with your paddle to activate. Timed effects last 8 seconds.

W

Wide Paddle

Expands your paddle to 1.5× its normal width. Makes it easier to catch the ball and power-ups.

⏱ 8 seconds
+

Extra Life

Adds one extra life, up to a maximum of 5. Grab these whenever you can!

⚡ Instant
M

Multi-Ball

Spawns 2 extra balls from the main ball's position. Extra balls glow orange. They disappear when they fall off screen.

🔥 Until lost
S

Slow Ball

Reduces ball speed to 60% of normal. Gives you more time to react and aim your bounces.

⏱ 8 seconds

Strategy

Tips & Tricks

🎯

Use the paddle edges. Hitting the ball near the edge of the paddle sends it at a sharp angle — great for reaching bricks in corners.

🧲

Prioritize green "+" power-ups. Extra lives are the most valuable power-up. Always try to catch them, even if it's risky.

🐢

Slow Ball is your friend. The purple "S" power-up makes everything easier. Use the slower speed to set up precise angle shots.

🔮

Multi-Ball is chaos. When you activate Multi-Ball, focus on keeping the main white ball alive. The orange extras are a bonus, not a priority.

🏰

On Fortress (Level 3): Focus on breaking the bottom wall first. Once you create a gap, the ball can bounce around inside and clear the core quickly.

⏸️

Pause when overwhelmed. Press ESC to pause the game, take a breath, and plan your next move.

📱

Mobile tip: Hold your phone in portrait mode. Keep your finger slightly below the paddle — this way you can see where you're aiming without your finger blocking the view.
